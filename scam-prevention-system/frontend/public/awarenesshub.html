Warning: Fake Delivery Packages Requiring Payment

Scammers are sending SMS messages claiming a package is held due to an unpaid shipping fee. Do not click the link. This tactic aims to harvest credit card details under the guise of a minor administrative charge.

Alert: AI Voice Clone Phone Calls

Criminals are using AI to mimic relatives' voices to ask for urgent money transfers. Always verify through another channel. These deepfake audio attacks are highly convincing and often target grandparents by mimicking grandchildren in distress.

Urgent: New Tax Refund Phishing Emails

Fraudsters are emailing victims claiming a tax refund is ready. This is a tactic to steal bank credentials. The emails often use official government logos and urgent language to trick users into logging into a cloned banking portal.
